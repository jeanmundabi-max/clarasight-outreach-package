The Architecture

5 GTM Pillars

Each pillar targets a different failure point in the enterprise sales motion. Together they create a compounding revenue system — not a one-time outreach blast.

01
Buying Window Intelligence

Map the full enterprise travel analytics addressable market across five buying window types. Produce a live list of companies in active buying windows — not a broad list of travel managers, but companies where something happened to make them active buyers this quarter.

Key Performance Indicators
  • 30+ named accounts mapped per quarter
  • 100% have confirmed buying signal evidence
  • Lead list refreshed on a 30-day cycle
LinkedIn Jobs SEC EDGAR 8-K CMS Open Payments CDP database Crunchbase M&A
02
Segment-Specific Outreach

Write messages built for the specific buying window — not a generic travel analytics pitch. A message to a new Head of Travel on day 14 of her job is different from a message to a Chief Financial Officer at a pharma company where the March 31 HCP reconciliation just revealed how broken their data is.

Key Performance Indicators
  • 5 distinct message variants (one per buying window type)
  • PSS score 85+ on all sequences
  • Reply rate target 3 to 5%
SmartLead LinkedIn Clay
03
Qualification and Sales Intelligence

Separate programmes with a live evaluation from those aware of the problem but not yet comparing vendors. Brief Clarasight's sales team before each call: what the programme looks like, what is broken, what the specific buying window is.

Key Performance Indicators
  • Pre-call brief delivered for every qualified conversation
  • Buying window classification confirmed before handoff
  • Decision-maker seniority verified before outreach
HubSpot Clay Apollo
04
Authority Engine

The compounding trust layer that ensures enterprise buyers encounter Clarasight during informal research — not after the shortlist has formed. LinkedIn content flywheel, 20-week value email nurture, micro-budget retargeting, lead scoring, and hot bucket alerts.

Key Performance Indicators
  • 8 to 12 LinkedIn posts per month
  • 20-week nurture sequence active for all engaged non-ready contacts
  • $5 to $10 per day retargeting spend
  • Hot alert delivered same day intent threshold crossed
HubSpot LinkedIn Campaign Manager Lead scoring model
05
Referral Engineering

Each Business Travel News 100 win becomes an active referral trigger, not a passive hope. Identify which existing customers are referral-ready — high Net Promoter Score signals, recent renewal, Global Business Travel Association community active. Engineer the ask with a specific referral incentive and a named peer programme as the target.

Key Performance Indicators
  • Referral ask rate 100% of renewing accounts
  • 1 referral introduction per quarter per active customer
  • Case study produced from each Business Travel News 100 win
  • Referral programme tracked in HubSpot by quarter
HubSpot Global Business Travel Association network Case study production workflow
The Opening Move

10-Day Outreach Sequence

Day-by-day execution for the first outreach cycle. Every touch uses a different channel and angle. No two messages make the same point.

Day Channel Action Content and Intent
Day 0 Email Trigger email sent "Sanofi. GSK. Clifford Chance." — Series A stage shift observation + buying window list offer. First email sets the frame: Clarasight is now competing at a level where informal shortlists form before the RFP lands.
Day 1 LinkedIn Connection request Business Travel News 100 win rate observation paired with a value-based connection note. No pitch. Build presence in the feed before any ask lands.
Day 3 LinkedIn Follow-up DM Buying window list offer — 20+ named accounts currently in active evaluation. Reinforces the free value frame introduced on Day 0. Concrete and specific.
Day 6 Email Friction email sent "Before the shortlist forms" — the informal shortlist formation problem. Enterprise buyers build mental shortlists 6 to 8 weeks before any formal process. A different angle from Day 0.
Day 7 Review Check reply rates If open rate is below 40%, review subject lines and sending infrastructure. If reply rate is below 1%, review message-to-segment fit before continuing. Adjust before Day 8.
Day 8 LinkedIn Breakup note "I'll leave the list with you regardless." Low-friction exit that leaves the door open. Sends buying window list whether or not there has been a reply. No pressure, no passive aggression.
Day 12 Email Permissionless Value email "HCP travel in 5 pharma" — CMS Open Payments intelligence for 5 named pharma companies. The email IS the value: a live finding from public data that quantifies the reconciliation gap, delivered before asking for anything.
Day 14 Review Qualification call brief Brief Clarasight sales team on every qualified responder to date. Buying window type, company profile, what was said in the reply, what they already know. Sales team walks in prepared, not cold.
Day 21 HubSpot Nurture entry All non-responders enter the 20-week Authority Engine nurture sequence in HubSpot. Pure value delivery for 20 weeks. No pitches. Hot bucket alert fires when intent threshold is crossed.
Day 30 Review Cycle review Which segments and messages produced conversations? Which buying windows had the strongest open-to-reply conversion? Refine the list, update message variants, and launch cycle 2 with what worked.
The Build

12-Week Roadmap

From zero to a fully running outbound and Authority Engine system. Each phase has a clear output. No vague milestones.

Weeks 1–2
Research and Targeting
  • Map full addressable market across all 5 buying window segments
  • Produce Tier 1 lead list — 12 accounts with confirmed Data Key evidence
  • Identify decision-maker contacts for each account
  • Verify all contacts are currently in role before loading
  • Load into HubSpot, Clay, and Apollo
Weeks 3–4
Outreach Build
  • Write 5 buying-window-specific outreach sequences (PSS score 85+)
  • Load sequences into SmartLead — campaigns created paused
  • LinkedIn connection requests begin for Tier 1 accounts
  • Run sending infrastructure deliverability check before activating
  • Review and activate campaigns after final quality pass
Weeks 5–7
First Conversations
  • Email outreach live across all 5 buying window segments
  • Monitor reply rates daily — target 3 to 5% per segment
  • Qualify responses and brief sales team before each call
  • First conversations expected by Week 6
  • Permissionless Value email deployed to top pharma segment on Day 12
Weeks 8–9
Optimise
  • Review which segments produce highest reply-to-conversation rate
  • Activate Tier 2 accounts using Tier 1 learnings
  • Authority Engine nurture begins for all non-ready contacts
  • Update message variants for underperforming segments
  • Retargeting ads live on LinkedIn for engaged non-converters
Weeks 10–12
Scale and Systemise
  • Scale winning segments to full total addressable market
  • Authority Engine fully running — content, nurture, retargeting, scoring, alerts
  • End-of-quarter report: conversations generated, pipeline created, improvements for quarter 2
  • Referral engineering begins with highest Net Promoter Score existing accounts
  • All Business Travel News 100 wins documented as active referral triggers
  • Buying window intelligence system handed to Clarasight for self-service use
